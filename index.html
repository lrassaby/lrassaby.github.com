Hiya! I'm Louis.

I'm an Aussie-American software engineer and cellist living in the East Village.

Coding for me is all about the creative process, and I like making things that align with my other interests, like a musical programming language or a 3D art installation. Above all, I love using computers to solve big problems.

I'm currently working as a software engineer at Recombine, a genetics startup in the Flatiron area. To learn more about me, check out my resume or connect with me on LinkedIn.

Away from the computer, I love to create things. Music is my biggest passion and I play a weekly gig with my friends at Caffe Vivaldi in the West Village.
